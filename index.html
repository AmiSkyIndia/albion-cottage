Welcome to

Your Home Away From Home

Nestled in a lush green garden in the peaceful Kaimbwala area of New Chandigarh, Albion Cottage is a private, fully self-contained retreat perfect for families, couples, and small groups.

Hosted by Rakhi — a warm, full-time host who loves gardening, cooking, and making every guest feel truly at home — the cottage offers a rare blend of privacy, comfort, and nature.
